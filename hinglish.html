Mumbai se Makkah — Hanafi Fiqh: Fard, Wajib, Sunnah & Mustahabb

Mumbai se Makkah tak ka ye safar Hanafi Fiqh ke mutabiq hai. Is guide mein Fard (compulsory), Wajib (chhootne par Dam), Sunnah aur Mustahabb (recommended) ka farq diya gaya hai.

1 Preparation (Mumbai Mein)

Safar shuru karne se pehle ye kaam ghar par hi kar lene chahiye.

  • Ihram ka Kapda: Mardon (men) ke liye niche Towel wala kapda (Izar) aur upar Cotton ya Linen ki chaadar (Rida) sabse best rehti hai.
  • Video Links (How to wear Ihram):
    • English: youtu.be/p3SgrvVjQ10
    • Hindi/Urdu: youtu.be/BgygBjDEkBg
    • Hinglish: youtu.be/IXmnpxcbHH0
  • Mustahabb Nakhun (nails) kaatna, ghair-zaruri baal saaf karna aur moonchh (mustache) trim karna.
  • Sunnah Ihram ki niyyat se nahaana (Ghusl). (Agar koi khatoon periods mein hain, toh wo bhi safai ke liye naha sakti hain).
  • Mustahabb Niyyat karne se pehle badan par (sir aur dadhi par) accha Attar lagana. Yaad rakhein: Ihram ki chaadron par perfume nahi lagana, sirf apne jism par lagayein.
  • Sunnah Ghar se nikalte waqt 2 Rak'at Nafl namaz padhna.
  • Important Rules: Agar bache saath hain, toh unki Niyyah unke Parents karenge. Agar ladies impure (periods) state mein hain, toh wo sirf Ghusl karein aur Ihram pehnein, lekin 2 Rak'at namaz na padhein. Wo hotel mein wait karein aur paak hone ke baad hi Umrah karein.

2 Miqat aur Niyyah (Flight Mein)

Mumbai se Jeddah ki flight mein "Yalamlam" (Miqat) landing se takreeban 30 mins pehle aata hai.

  • Ihram ki 2 bina sili chaadre (unstitched cloths) aap Mumbai airport par ya flight mein shuru mein hi pehen lein.
  • Fard Sirf kapde pehenne se Ihram shuru nahi hota. Jab Miqat aane wala ho (airline announce karti hai), tab Niyyat + Talbiyah dono zaruri hain.
  • Warning: Airport par board karte waqt Talbiyah bilkul na padhein, warna sari paabandiyan wahin se shuru ho jayengi.
  • Sone se pehle Niyyah: Flight mein aksar khana khane ke baad log so jate hain. Behtar hai ki aap sone se pehle hi Niyyat aur Talbiyah padh lein, kyunki agar neend mein Miqat nikal gaya toh 'Dam' (penalty) dena padega.
  • Talbiyah ki Awaaz: Mard (men) thodi buland (loud) awaaz mein Talbiyah padhein, aur ladies itni halki awaaz mein ki sirf wo khud sun sakein.

Niyyah (Arabic)

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أُرِيدُ الْعُمْرَةَ فَيَسِّرْهَا لِي وَتَقَبَّلْهَا مِنِّي

O Allah, I intend Umrah, make it easy for me and accept it from me.

Talbiyah (Arabic)

لَبَّيْكَ اللَّهُمَّ لَبَّيْكَ، لَبَّيْكَ لاَ شَرِيكَ لَكَ لَبَّيْكَ، إِنَّ الْحَمْدَ، وَالنِّعْمَةَ، لَكَ وَالْمُلْكَ، لاَ شَرِيكَ لَكَ

Labbayk Allahumma labbayk — Here I am, O Allah, You have no partner...

3 Ihram ki 8 Paabandiyan (Strict Rules)

Niyyat karte hi ye 8 paabandiyan lagu ho jati hain.

  1. Sar aur Chehra: Mardon ke liye sar aur chehra dhakna mana hai. Ladies ke liye chehra khula hona chahiye, kapda skin ko touch na kare (wo cap wala naqab use kar sakti hain). Ladies jewellery pehen sakti hain.
  2. Perfume: Dono ke liye khushbu, attar ya scented soap mana hai.
  3. Baal aur Nakhun: Body ke kisi bhi part ke baal todna/kaatna ya nakhun kaatna mana hai.
  4. Stitched Clothes: Mardon ke liye koi bhi sila hua kapda (underwear, shirt etc.) mana hai. Ladies normal sile hue kapde pehen sakti hain.
  5. Marital Activity: Miyan-biwi ka koi bhi aisi activity ya baat-cheet mana hai.
  6. Footwear: Mardon ko aisi chappal pehenni hai jisme pair ke upar ki haddi (instep bone) khuli rahe. Band joote mana hain.
  7. Greenery: Haram ki boundary mein ghaas, patti ya phool todna mana hai.
  8. Shikar: Kisi bhi janwar ka shikar ya usko marna (yahan tak ki sar ki joon ya kida) mana hai.

4 Makkah aur Haram mein Entry

  • Hudood-e-Haram: Jab aap boundary par 'Rehal' wala gate dekhein, toh ye Dua padhein: "Allahumma hadha haramuka wa amnuka faharrim lahmi wa dami 'alan-nar."
  • Rest: Makkah pahunch kar pehle hotel jana aur aaraam (rest) karna bilkul sahi hai taaki aap fresh ho kar Umrah karein.
  • Masjid Entry: Seedha pair andar rakhein aur ye Dua padhein: "Allahummaf-tah li abwaba rahmatik."
  • Pehli Nazar: Kaaba par pehli nazar padte hi Dua qubool hoti hai.
  • Tip: Nazar niche rakh kar andar jayein. Ek side mein aisi jagah khade hon jahan bheed na ho aur aap der tak Dua kar sakein. Phir Kaaba dekh kar apni saari haajatein maangein.
  • Stop Talbiyah: Kaaba dekhne ke baad aur Tawaf shuru karne se pehle Talbiyah padhna band kar dein.

5 Tawaf (Kaaba ke chakkar)

Masjid-al-Haram pahunch kar Wudu karein aur Tawaf shuru karein.

  • Sunnah Idtiba (Men): Tawaf ke 7 rounds mein right shoulder khula rakhein (chadar baghal ke niche se nikal kar).
  • Niyyah: Hajar-al-Aswad ki line se thoda pehle khade hon ki Kaaba samne ho. Niyyah karein: "Allahumma inni uridu tawafa baytikal haram fayas-sirhu li wa taqabbalha minni."
  • Sunnah Istilam: Line par aayein, 'Bismillahi Allahu Akbar' kahein aur dono hatheliyon se ishara karke unhe choom lein (awaz na nikalein).
  • Fard 7 counter-clockwise chakkar.
  • Sunnah Raml (men): Pehle 3 rounds mein thoda akad kar, kandhe hila kar aur tez qadmon se chalein. Baaki 4 rounds normal chalein.
  • Gaze (Nazar): Chalte waqt nazar niche (pairon ki taraf) rakhein. Kaaba ko ghurna ya idhar-udhar dekhna Makruh-e-Tanzihi hai.
  • Rules: Agar Tawaf ke beech Namaz shuru ho jaye ya Wudu toot jaye, toh wahin ruk jayein. Namaz ya Wudu ke baad bilkul usi jagah se continue karein jahan choda tha.

6 Post-Tawaf aur Zamzam

  • Shoulder: Tawaf ke baad kandha dhak lein.
  • Wajib 2 Rak'at Namaz: Ye Wajib hai. Maqam-e-Ibrahim ke peeche ya haram mein kahin bhi padhein.
  • Mustahabb Zamzam: Kaaba ki taraf munh karke pet bhar kar (full stomach) piyein aur ye Dua padhein: "Allahumma inni as'aluka 'ilman nafi'an..."
  • IMP (9th Istilam): Sa'i ke liye jane se pehle dobara Hajar-al-Aswad ki line par ja kar ek aur baar Istilam karna Sunnah hai.

7 Sa'i (Safa aur Marwa)

  • Safa Pahadi: Itni height par chadein jahan se Kaaba dikhe.
  • Niyyah: "Allahumma inni uridus-sa'ya... fayas-sirhu li wa taqabbalha minni."
  • Zikr: Chalte waqt 4th Kalma aur ye Dua padhein: "Rabbighfir warham, wa tajaawaz 'amma ta'lam, innaka antal a'azzul akram."
  • Dua: Safa aur Marwa dono pahadiyon par har chakkar mein haath utha kar Dua karein.
  • Masle: Sa'i hamesha Tawaf ke baad hoti hai. Agar thak jayein toh break le sakte hain, lekin ek baar mein khatam karna behtar hai.
  • Wajib Halq/Taqsir: Mardon ke liye pura sar mundwana (Halq) best hai, warna kam se kam 1/4th (one-quarter) sar ke baal 1 inch katwayein. Ladies sirf 1 inch baal niche se katwaein.

All Duas in One Place

Performing Umrah is a deeply spiritual experience, and the "tongue of the believer" is the greatest tool during this journey. In Hanafi Fiqh there are specific Duas from the Sunnah; you are also encouraged to ask for your personal needs in your own language.

1. Niyyah for Umrah (At the time of Ihram)

Recite when you are ready to enter Ihram before the Miqat.

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أُرِيدُ الْعُمْرَةَ فَيَسِّرْهَا لِي وَتَقَبَّلْهَا مِنِّي

Allahumma inni uridu al-'umrata fa-yassirha li wa taqabbalha minni.

O Allah, I intend to perform Umrah, so make it easy for me and accept it from me.

2. The Talbiyah (The Anthem of the Pilgrim)

Recite constantly after Niyyah until you reach the Kaaba.

لَبَّيْكَ اللَّهُمَّ لَبَّيْكَ، لَبَّيْكَ لاَ شَرِيكَ لَكَ لَبَّيْكَ، إِنَّ الْحَمْدَ، وَالنِّعْمَةَ، لَكَ وَالْمُلْكَ، لاَ شَرِيكَ لَكَ

Labbayk Allahumma labbayk, labbayka la sharika laka labbayk. Innal hamda, wan-ni'mata, laka wal mulk, la sharika lak.

Here I am, O Allah, here I am. Here I am, You have no partner, here I am. Verily all praise and blessings are Yours, and all sovereignty, You have no partner.

3. Entering the Haram / Makkah Boundary

Recite when you see the "Rehal" boundary markers.

اللَّهُمَّ هَذَا حَرَمُكَ وَأَمْنُكَ فَحَرِّمْ لَحْمِي وَدَمِي عَلَى النَّارِ

Allahumma hadha haramuka wa amnuka faharrim lahmi wa dami 'alan-nar.

O Allah, this is Your sanctuary and security, so make my flesh and blood forbidden to the Fire.

4. Entering Masjid al-Haram

Step in with your right foot.

بِسْمِ اللهِ وَالصَّلَاةُ وَالسَّلَامُ عَلَى رَسُولِ اللهِ، اللَّهُمَّ افْتَحْ لِي أَبْوَابَ رَحْمَتِكَ

Bismillahi was-salatu was-salamu 'ala rasulillahi, Allahummaf-tah li abwaba rahmatik.

In the name of Allah, peace and blessings be upon the Messenger of Allah. O Allah, open the doors of Your mercy for me.

5. At the First Sight of the Kaaba

There is no fixed wording; it is Sunnah to say the following, then make your personal Duas (they are accepted at this moment).

اللهُ أَكْبَرُ، لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللهُ

Allahu Akbar, La ilaha illallah.

Allah is Great, there is no god but Allah. (Follow with your personal heartfelt Duas.)

6. Starting Tawaf (At the Black Stone)

Recite at the Green Light line during Istilam.

بِسْمِ اللهِ، اللهُ أَكْبَرُ

Bismillahi, Allahu Akbar.

In the name of Allah, Allah is Great.

7. Between the Yemeni Corner and the Black Stone

Recite during the last part of every circuit of Tawaf.

رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ

Rabbana atina fid-dunya hasanatan wa fil-akhirati hasanatan wa qina 'adhaban-nar.

Our Lord, give us good in this world and good in the Hereafter, and protect us from the punishment of the Fire.

8. After Drinking Zamzam

Face the Kaaba and drink.

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ عِلْمًا نَافِعًا، وَرِزْقًا وَاسِعًا، وَشِفَاءً مِنْ كُلِّ دَاءٍ

Allahumma inni as'aluka 'ilman nafi'an, wa rizqan wasi'an, wa shifa'an min kulli da'.

O Allah, I ask You for beneficial knowledge, abundant provision, and a cure from every illness.

9. Niyyah for Sa'i (At Mount Safa)

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أُرِيدُ السَّعْيَ بَيْنَ الصَّفَا وَالْمَرْوَةِ سَبْعَةَ أَشْوَاطٍ لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى فَيَسِّرْهُ لِي وَتَقَبَّلْهُ مِنِّي

Allahumma inni uridus-sa'ya baynas-safa wal marwati sab'ata ashwatin lillahi ta'ala fayas-sirhu li wa taqabbalhu minni.

O Allah, I intend to perform seven rounds of Sa'i between Safa and Marwa for Your sake, so make it easy for me and accept it from me.

10. During the Walk of Sa'i (Especially between Green Lights)

رَبِّ اغْفِرْ وَارْحَمْ، وَتَجَاوَزْ عَمَّا تَعْلَمْ، إِنَّكَ أَنْتَ الْأَعَزُّ الْأَكْرَمُ

Rabbighfir warham, wa tajaawaz 'amma ta'lam, innaka antal a'azzul akram.

My Lord, forgive and have mercy, and overlook what You know (of my sins); verily You are the Most Mighty, the Most Noble.

11. General Zikr during Sa'i (The 4th Kalimah)

Recite frequently while walking between Safa and Marwa.

لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ، لَهُ الْمُلْكُ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ، وَهُوَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ

La ilaha illallahu wahdahu la sharika lah, lahul mulku wa lahul hamdu, wa huwa 'ala kulli shai'in qadeer.

There is no god but Allah, alone, without partner. His is the sovereignty, and His is the praise, and He has power over all things.

12. Leaving Masjid al-Haram

Step out with your left foot.

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ مِنْ فَضْلِكَ

Allahumma inni as'aluka min fadlik.

O Allah, I ask You for Your favor/bounty.

Final tip: Carry these on your phone or print them out. While these Arabic Duas are beautiful, Allah (SWT) knows the language of your heart. Don't forget to make Dua for your family, the Ummah, and your own forgiveness in your native language.

Dam (Penalty) — Note

Agar koi Wajib kaam (jaise Miqat par niyyat na karna, ya 2 Rak'at namaz na padhna, ya Sa'i na karna) chhoot jaye, toh ek bakre ki qurbani (Dam) Makkah mein deni padti hai.

Sunna ya Mustahabb chhootne par koi Dam nahi; sirf sawab kam. Agar Fard (jaise Tawaf) chhoot jaye toh Umrah invalid ho jata hai.
